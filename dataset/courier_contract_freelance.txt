1. Services Provided
The Contractor agrees to provide food and/or goods delivery services to customers of the Company ("Services"). The Contractor shall perform the Services as an independent contractor and not as an employee of the Company. The Contractor retains sole discretion over the manner and means of performing the Services, including the routes taken and the working hours, subject to the terms of this Agreement.

2. Compensation and Payment
The Company shall compensate the Contractor for the Services rendered based on the terms outlined in the Company's delivery payment schedule, which include a hourly base fee per delivery of 20 euro, mileage fee of 0.5 euro, and tips. The Company will provide a detailed breakdown of earnings on a monthly basis. The Contractor acknowledges that they are solely responsible for all tax liabilities, including but not limited to income tax and VAT.

3. Independent Contractor Status
The Contractor is an independent business owner and is not an employee, partner, or joint venturer of the Company. The Contractor is responsible for all costs and expenses incurred in performing the Services, including but not limited to vehicle maintenance, fuel, insurance, and personal equipment. The Company will not withhold any taxes from the Contractor's compensation.

4. Equipment and Uniform
The Contractor is required to provide and maintain their own delivery vehicle, and a smartphone with a reliable data plan. The Company may provide branded insulated bags and other equipment, which remain the property of the Company.

5. Term and Termination
This Agreement is effective as of $CONTRACT_DATE and will continue until terminated by either party. Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time, with or without cause, upon 30 days' written notice to the other party.

6. Confidentiality and Data Protection
The Contractor agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all Company business information, including customer data, trade secrets, and operational processes, both during and after the term of this Agreement. The Contractor must handle all personal data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

7. Indemnification and Liability
The Contractor agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Company, its officers, and its agents from any and all claims, damages, liabilities, and expenses (including legal fees) arising from the Contractor's performance of the Services. The Contractor is responsible for obtaining and maintaining appropriate insurance, including commercial vehicle insurance and professional liability insurance.

8. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the country where the services are performed (Germany, Netherlands, or UK).

9. Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, and negotiations.
